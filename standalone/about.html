About Me

Get to know the person behind the code

5+ Years Experience

A Passionate Developer Crafting Digital Experiences

Hello! I'm Michael Kariuki, a web developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I specialize in creating beautiful, functional, and user-centered digital experiences that help businesses grow and connect with their audiences.

My journey into web development began in 2019 when I built my first website for a local business. Since then, I've had the privilege of working with startups, small businesses, and established companies to bring their visions to life.

I believe that great websites are built on a foundation of clean code, thoughtful design, and a deep understanding of user needs. Every project I take on is an opportunity to create something meaningful that makes a real difference.
